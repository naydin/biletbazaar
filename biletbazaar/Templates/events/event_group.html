The success of Tarkan in the early 90s was seen by some as a sign of growing admiration for Western music, and the artist's real success came two years later. Collaborating with Turkey's "little sparrow", prominent producer and songwriter Sezen Aksu,[19] Tarkan's highly anticipated second album [Acayipsin]] (Oh - You're Something Else) sold more than two million copies in Turkey and 750 thousand copies in Europe.
